The Window

A monkey looked
out of the window.
Mocking, it said,
"Look at that—
an ugly monkey.
Look at its large ears.
Look at its ugly smile.
Look at its strange tail.
How ugly."

One teased the other.
The other teased back.
Annoyed, the monkey
threw a stone.

The mirror shattered.

The monkey laughed
at itself, and said:
"To be beautiful,
you must know
you are ugly."
